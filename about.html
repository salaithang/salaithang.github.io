About Me

Burmese Chin Community Of Atlanta is an organization established in 2008 to help the Burmese Chin Refugees settled in Atlanta,GA with their adjustments in the new society and also to maintain the culture and tradition they brought from their native land. I was one of the founding members of the organization and as an elected president for the next 2 years(2015-2016), I will be focusing to work with various NGO organizations to expand the social works among the Burmese Chin refugees.

Chin Community of USA is a nationwide non profit organization established in 2002. I worked as one the youth leaders there and elected as Youth secretary until I resigned from the post. I founded Chin Community Youth, USA during my time as youth secretary and was the president of the CCYUSA until I resigned from the post.

What will I go next

I will continue to study and advance my knowledge and skills. I will continue to help the community anyway I can and continue to grow in physically, Spriritually. Thank you.
